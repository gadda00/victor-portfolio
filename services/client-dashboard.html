How the journey works

From first idea to launched AI product — here's every step.

1

Scope

Use the Project Scope Wizard — 6 questions, 3 minutes. Get an intelligent package recommendation and instant price estimate.

2

Register

Your brief is saved to your browser. Add your name, email, and company. No password — your data stays on your device.

3

Contract

Generate a draft contract from your brief. Download as PDF. Review and sign when ready.

4

Pay

Choose your payment plan — 50/50, milestones, or monthly. Pay via M-Pesa, Flutterwave, or Stripe. See payment options.

5

Track

Watch your project move through milestones: Discovery → Build → Review → Launch. Status updates land here automatically.

6

Launch

Your AI tool goes live. Get training, documentation, and 30–90 days of support. Then we iterate on what users actually need.

Your data, your control

All briefs, contracts, and payment records are stored locally in your browser (localStorage). Nothing is sent to any server until you explicitly email or share a brief. Clearing your browser data removes everything.
