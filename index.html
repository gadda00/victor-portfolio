About

Turning data into intelligence.

I build production-grade AI systems that solve real problems.

I build AI systems that turn raw data into intelligence. My work lives at the intersection of multi-agent orchestration, real statistical math, and production reliability.

I'm the founder of Busara AI (busaraai.com) — a 50-agent data intelligence platform running a 7-stage parallel DAG pipeline with real math: Holt-Winters forecasting, Z-score/IQR/EWMA anomaly ensembles, OLS regression, Granger causality, K-Means clustering, and PII detection.

I'm also building KilimoPRO (kilimo.pro) — an AI-powered agricultural intelligence platform for Kenyan and African farmers, with on-device crop disease detection, market price forecasting, and multi-LLM advisory.

Based in Nairobi, building for Africa and beyond. 5+ years shipping production systems.
