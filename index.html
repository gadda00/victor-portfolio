About

Hi, I'm Victor — nice to meet you.

I'm an AI engineer and founder based in Nairobi, Kenya. I build practical AI systems that solve real problems — the kind that help a farmer detect crop disease on a $80 phone, or help a business turn messy data into clear decisions.

I founded Busara AI — a platform with 50 AI agents working together like a team of analysts, each handling a specific task (data gathering, analysis, forecasting, reporting). Think of it as a digital research department that runs 24/7. Try it live →

I also build KilimoPRO — an app that puts agricultural intelligence in farmers' hands. Take a photo of a diseased leaf, get an instant diagnosis and treatment plan. Check market prices before you sell. Get weather advisories in Swahili. All offline-capable. See the code →

I care about building things that actually work — not demos, not hype. Real math, real users, real impact. I believe Africa's AI future will be built by people who understand both the technology and the context. That's the work I wake up for.

Whether you're a small business owner curious about AI, a fellow developer, or someone with an ambitious idea — I'd love to hear from you. Let's build something that matters.
